Remaining PSL Matches Likely to be Played in Rawalpindi: Sources

Published on [Date] | PSL News

The Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB) is considering shifting the remaining matches of the Pakistan Super League (PSL) 2024 to Rawalpindi Cricket Stadium, according to insider sources.

The decision comes amid logistical and security assessments, with Rawalpindi emerging as the preferred venue due to its central location and proven track record of hosting high-profile matches.

Why Rawalpindi?

  • Weather Conditions: Better forecast compared to other cities.
  • Infrastructure: Well-equipped facilities for teams and broadcasters.
  • Security: Strong contingency plans in place.
  • Fan Engagement: High attendance expected in the cricket-loving city.

Potential Impact on Teams & Fixtures

If approved, the revised schedule may see:

  • Double-headers to accommodate the remaining games.
  • Adjusted travel plans for franchises.
  • Increased marketing push in Rawalpindi/Islamabad regions.

PCB Yet to Confirm

While sources indicate a strong likelihood, the PCB has not made an official announcement. A decision is expected within 48 hours after final reviews.
