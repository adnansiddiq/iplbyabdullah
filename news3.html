Daryl Mitchell Debunks Indian Media Reports with Heartfelt PSL Statement

Published on May 11, 2025 | Cricket News

New Zealand all-rounder Daryl Mitchell has dismissed misleading reports from certain sections of the Indian media, reaffirming his affection for Pakistan and its cricket fans.

The 32-year-old, who is currently playing in the Pakistan Super League (PSL), took to social media to clarify his stance after false narratives circulated about his views on playing in Pakistan.

Mitchell's Clear Stance on Pakistan

Mitchell, known for his explosive batting and handy medium-pace bowling, is representing the Karachi Kings in PSL 9. Responding to claims that he was reluctant to play in Pakistan, Mitchell set the record straight with a heartfelt statement.

"Pakistan is one of the countries closest to my heart. The love and support I've received from the fans here have been incredible. It's always a pleasure to play in front of such passionate crowds."

— Daryl Mitchell

His statement comes after certain Indian media outlets misrepresented his comments, suggesting he was unhappy about playing in Pakistan. Mitchell's clarification not only debunks these claims but also highlights the strong bond he shares with Pakistani cricket lovers.

A Valuable Asset in PSL

Since joining the Karachi Kings, Mitchell has been a key player, contributing with both bat and ball. His experience in various T20 leagues around the world, including the IPL, makes him a vital asset in the PSL. His positive remarks about Pakistan further strengthen the league's reputation as a welcoming and competitive tournament for international stars.

Fans Appreciate Mitchell's Honesty

Pakistani fans have praised Mitchell for his candid response and dedication to the game. Many took to social media to express their admiration, with some calling him a "true ambassador of the sport." His statement also reinforces the growing camaraderie between international players and Pakistan cricket, which has seen a resurgence in hosting top-tier talent since the return of home matches.

Conclusion

Daryl Mitchell's clarification serves as a reminder of the importance of authentic reporting in sports journalism. By openly expressing his love for Pakistan and its cricket culture, Mitchell has not only shut down false narratives but also endeared himself further to fans. As PSL 9 continues, his performances on the field—and his respect off it—will undoubtedly make him a fan favorite.

For now, Mitchell remains focused on delivering for Karachi Kings, proving once again that cricket transcends boundaries and unites players and fans alike.
