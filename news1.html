PSL 10: Quetta Gladiators Crush Islamabad United by 109 Runs

Date: May 07, 2025

Venue: Rawalpindi Cricket Stadium

Quetta Gladiators asserted their dominance in PSL 10 with a commanding 109-run victory over Islamabad United. Centuries from Rilee Rossouw (104 off 46) and Hasan Nawaz (100* off 45) propelled the Gladiators to a record-breaking total of 263/3.

Islamabad United's chase faltered from the start, losing both openers to Mohammad Amir in the first over. Despite brief resistance from Muhammad Shahzad (24 off 13) and Imad Wasim (56 off 41), United collapsed to 154 all out in 19.3 overs.

Bowling Highlights:

  • Mohammad Amir: 2/6 from 2 overs
  • Mohammad Wasim Jr and Abrar Ahmed: 2 wickets each

Top Performers:

  • Quetta Gladiators: Rilee Rossouw (104), Hasan Nawaz (100*)
  • Islamabad United: Imad Wasim (56), Ben Dwarshuis (31)

Quetta Gladiators continue to lead PSL 10, while Islamabad United faces their fourth straight defeat.
